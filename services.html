Project Terms

01

Timeline & Kickoff

Timelines apply to post-production by default and commence only after all raw assets, brief, and scope are finalized in writing.

02

Payment Terms

50% deposit before production. Remaining balance due before final delivery. Invoices are due within 7 business days of delivery.

03

Deliverables

Final assets provided in agreed platform formats. Project files and raw unedited footage (if shot by me) are not included unless scoped.

04

Revision Scope

Covers adjustments within the approved direction. Major concept changes require a revised scope. Additional rounds beyond the package limit are billed at IDR 150K / round (+IDR 200K for Brand Film, Long-Form Video & Launch Package).

05

Additional Requests

Any requests outside the initial scope will be quoted separately before execution.

06

Usage Rights

Fully licensed for client use upon full payment. I retain portfolio showcase rights.

07

Project Cancellation

Deposits are non-refundable once production begins. Completed work will be billed accordingly.
